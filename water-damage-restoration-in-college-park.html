When To Call

Signs You Need Water Damage Restoration

Recognizing the signs of water damage is crucial for timely intervention. If you notice any of these indicators, it's essential to contact a Water Damage Restoration service in College Park immediately.

1. Visible water stains on walls or ceilings

Water stains indicate that there is a leak or water intrusion issue that needs to be addressed to prevent further damage.

2. Musty odors in your home

A musty smell often signals mold growth due to moisture, which can pose health risks if not treated quickly.

3. Peeling or bubbling paint

This can indicate trapped moisture behind the walls, suggesting a water leak that requires immediate attention.

4. Increased humidity levels indoors

High humidity can lead to mold growth and indicates that there may be a water issue that needs to be resolved.

5. Warped or buckling flooring

If your floors appear warped, it may be a sign of water damage beneath, requiring professional assessment and restoration.
