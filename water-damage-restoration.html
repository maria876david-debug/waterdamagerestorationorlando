Our Water Damage Restoration Process

Understanding our Water Damage Restoration process helps you know what to expect during service.

1

Inspection

We start with a thorough inspection of your property to assess the extent of the water damage. This helps us develop a tailored restoration plan.

2

Water Extraction

Using industrial-strength water extraction pumps, we remove standing water from your home. This step is critical to prevent further damage.

3

Drying

Our team uses dehumidifiers and air movers to dry affected areas thoroughly. This process is essential for preventing mold growth.

4

Cleaning and Sanitizing

We clean and sanitize all affected areas, using mold-resistant paint and other products to ensure your home is safe and healthy.

5

Restoration

Finally, we restore your property to its original condition, repairing drywall, flooring, and any other damaged materials.
