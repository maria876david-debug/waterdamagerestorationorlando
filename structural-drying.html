Understanding Structural Drying in Orlando

Structural Drying in Orlando involves the systematic removal of moisture from building materials and structures after water damage. This process typically employs specialized equipment like dehumidifiers and air movers to expedite drying and prevent mold growth. Our team utilizes advanced tools, including moisture meters and infrared cameras, to ensure thorough drying.

Local Orlando customers often face unique challenges due to the region's humidity and frequent rain. Whether dealing with water leaks from plumbing issues or flooding from heavy storms, timely structural drying is essential. Our affordable drying services in Orlando are designed to address these scenarios, ensuring homes remain safe and dry.

Our Structural Drying Process

Our step-by-step process ensures efficient and effective structural drying in Orlando.

1

Assessment

We begin with a thorough assessment of the affected areas using moisture meters to determine the extent of the damage.

2

Water Extraction

Our team uses powerful water extraction pumps to remove standing water, ensuring a dry environment for the drying process.

3

Setup Equipment

We strategically place dehumidifiers and air movers to facilitate airflow and expedite moisture removal from the structure.

4

Monitoring

Throughout the process, we continuously monitor humidity levels and adjust equipment as necessary to ensure optimal drying conditions.

5

Final Inspection

Once drying is complete, we conduct a final inspection to ensure all areas are thoroughly dried and safe for occupancy.
